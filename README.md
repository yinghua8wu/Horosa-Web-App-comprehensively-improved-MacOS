## Signature Workflows / 代表性工作流

### Natal to Timing / 从本命到时运

Horosa is already structured for a continuous predictive workflow: start from natal and 3D chart reading, then move into primary directions, zodiacal releasing, firdaria, profection, solar arc, returns, and annual methods without leaving the same desktop product.

星阙已经把从本命盘到推运体系的链路接成一体：本命盘、三维盘、主/界限法、黄道星释、法达、小限、太阳弧、返照与流年法不是散模块，而是连续的时运阅读工作流。

### Relationship Analysis / 关系分析

The relationship layer is not a single compare screen. It already covers compare, composite, synastry, time-space midpoint, and Marks charts as parallel ways to inspect the same pair or case.

关系分析也不是单页比较盘，而是比较盘、组合盘、影响盘、时空中点盘、马克斯盘并行存在，用来从不同结构看同一段关系或同一组个案。

### Chinese Traditional Stack / 中国传统术数栈

Horosa already includes Bazi, Ziwei, gua-symbol references, twelve-palace tools, calendar, and Feng Shui in the same desktop environment, which makes the product feel like a broader Chinese traditional stack rather than a single-method tool.

八字、紫微斗数、八卦类象、十二串宫、万年历与风水已经都落在同一个桌面工作面里，所以它更像一套中国传统术数栈，而不是某一术的单点工具。

### Yi & Sanshi Depth / 易与三式纵深

Yi and Sanshi are represented both as standalone modules and as a deeper integrated surface: Su Zhan, Yi Gua, Liu Ren, Jin Kou, Dun Jia, Tai Yi, Tong She Fa, and Sanshi United with overview, shensha, references, and Bagong details.

易与三式部分既有单术入口，也有纵深整合面：宿盘、易卦、六壬、金口诀、遁甲、太乙、统摄法，以及带概览、神煞、六壬参考和八宫详解的三式合一。

## Capability Matrix / 功能矩阵

### Western Astrology / 西方占星

From natal charting to predictive timing and relationship analysis, the macOS build already exposes natal, 3D, directional, return, annual, and comparative surfaces in one app.

- Natal chart and 3D chart
- Primary directions, zodiacal releasing, firdaria, profection, solar arc, returns, annual methods
- Compare, composite, synastry, time-space midpoint, and Marks charts

### Global and Specialty Modules / 全球与专门模块

Horosa also pushes beyond the standard Western stack with modules that make the workspace feel unusually broad for a desktop release.

- Jieqi charts
- Astrocartography and planetary maps
- Qizheng Siyu, Hellenistic, Indian, and quantitative views

### Chinese Traditional and Divination / 中国传统与术数

The Chinese traditional layer is structured as a system rather than a decorative add-on.

- Bazi, Ziwei, gua-symbol references, twelve-palace tools, and rule references
- Calendar and Feng Shui as first-class modules
- Yi and Sanshi modules: Su Zhan, Yi Gua, Liu Ren, Jin Kou, Dun Jia, Tai Yi, Tong She Fa

### Desktop Workflow / 桌面工作流

The app is not only about calculation. It already includes the controls needed to shape, filter, inspect, and export research-oriented reading sessions.

- Chart configuration, aspect selection, planet selection
- Chart components and utility tools
- AI export and AI export settings
