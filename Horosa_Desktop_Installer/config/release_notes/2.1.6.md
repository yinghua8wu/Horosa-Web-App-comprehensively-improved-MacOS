- **奇门月柱交节边界修复**:月柱改按精确交节时刻判定。交节当日、交节时刻之前出生不再被误算进新月(立春同时校正年柱)。例:2005-05-05 16:30 立夏(17:52)前 → 月柱庚辰(原误作辛巳)。
- **Qimen month-pillar boundary fixed**: the month pillar now switches at the exact solar-term instant. A time before the term's crossing on the crossing day is no longer pushed into the next month (Lichun also rolls the year pillar). E.g. 2005-05-05 16:30, before Lixia (17:52) → month pillar 庚辰 (previously mis-set to 辛巳).
- **奇门置闰(超神接气置闰)定局修复**:置闰排盘改用经超神/接气/置闰校正后的节气定局,符头超前节气时提前换局并正确标注节气。例:2027-10-31 → 立冬上元六局(原误标霜降)。拆补法不受影响。
- **Qimen leap-method (超神接气置闰) chart-setup fixed**: leap-method casting now derives the chart from the solar term after 超神/接气/置闰 correction, advancing the chart when the lead-day precedes the term and labeling the term correctly. E.g. 2027-10-31 → 立冬 upper-yuan ju-6 (previously mislabeled 霜降). The 拆补 method is unaffected.
- **印度占星盘地图选点修复**:在地图上选定地点并确认后不再报错,经纬度正常带入排盘。
- **India astrology map location-pick fixed**: selecting a location on the map and confirming no longer throws; longitude/latitude now flow into the chart correctly.
